Getting to Know Lupita Nyong'o

  • Lupita Nyong’o in full is Lupita Amondi Nyong’o. She was born March 1, 1983, in Mexico City. She is a Kenyan actress who has won an Academy Award for her film debut in 12 Years a Slave (2013).

  • She attended Hampshire College, Amherst, Massachusetts, and, while still a student, she served as a production assistant and an intern on several movies. In 2007 she graduated with a bachelor’s degree in film and African studies. Her senior thesis, the film In My Genes (2009), was featured at the 2010 New York African Film Festival.

  • Nyong’o later assumed a role in the television miniseries Shuga (2009; 2012), about youth culture in Lagos. She then enrolled at the Yale School of Drama, where she earned a master’s degree in acting (2012).
